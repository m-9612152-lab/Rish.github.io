Our Story

About Dynamic Cleaning Services

Raising the standard of professional cleaning in Klang Valley to the best.

Dynamic Dream Builders Sdn Bhd was established with a bold vision: to deliver world-class cleaning services to the Klang Valley at every price point. We believe that clean environments aren't a luxury — they're a foundation for productivity, health, and well-being.

Our team of over 15 trained cleaning professionals is the backbone of everything we do. Each member undergoes rigorous training in cleaning SOPs, customer service, and safety protocols before they ever enter a client's premises. We invest in our people because we know that great results start with great people.

We hold active licences from the Ministry of Finance for both Cleaning Services (221001) and Landscaping (221401), reflecting our commitment to operating with full transparency and professionalism in all that we do.

Today, Dynamic serves over 80 regular clients ranging from luxury condominiums and corporate offices to private residences and post-construction sites across Kuala Lumpur, Petaling Jaya, Subang Jaya, Shah Alam, and the wider Klang Valley.
